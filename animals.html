Lola

We never really had a proper house type cat, all we had was a few outdoor and wild type cats. I would always beg my parents for just one house cat as it would be nice to have something to take care of, and becuase i can relate to a cat! Well getting Lola was probably one of the best accidents that could happen, My older siser had asked one of her friends to bring down a kitten from mitchelstown when she was coming to visit the next week. My mother didnt like this at all, My sister was told to tell the friend not to bother. My sister managed to "Forget" so after being in work all day I got a call to say that there was a cat at home and not to tell my parents yet. In the end, I managed to gain a cat and my parents got a free pass on my birthday present.
